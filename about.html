//Mission

Printing “Hello, world!” in a console is the first thing that people do when they learn a new programming language. Just like the meaning behind the phrase, we want to provide support for students -- no matter their proficiency in technological concepts -- to learn to stand their ground in the vast field of tech.

We aim to foster an environment where individuals passionate about technology can help improve digital literacy and accessibility. We focus on preparing students for the oncoming era and providing opportunities to utilize their talents.

//Initiatives

1. VS Coding Exploratory;

We are leading a VS exploratory that teaches students about basic coding concepts using Scratch, every Monday 2:45~3:35.


2. Fundraisers;

We plan on hosting fundraisers inside the school so that we can support individuals without access to technology by providing them with devices and appropriate digital tools.


3. Software Development;

We are programming our club’s website/portfolio with HTML/CSS/JavaScript. We also plan on developing software that educates children on technological literacy.



//Divisions


1. Communications Management;

The Communications Management manages our Instagram. The division members promote our club through various social media outlets.


2. VS Exploratory Helpers;

The VS Exploratory Helpers attend the exploratory sessions every Monday 2:45~3:35 and help teach the VS students.


3. Website Management;

The Website Management documents projects on our website and updates information as needed.


4. Members;

Members will take part in organizing and executing any of the other events such as fundraisers.
